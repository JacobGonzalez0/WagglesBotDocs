What is a Waggles?

WagglesBot is a bot for making DerpiBooru searches!

She can process and post search results right in the chat, with a simple ~d pinkie pie In addition she knows when DerpiBooru links are posted and can retrieve info such as the artist as well as all relevant tags. Her primary use lies in her ability to remember channel specific links and images, reducing the complexity of running commands. Chains can be created, a simple derpi search can be followed up with a ~tags or ~artist with no arguments. Waggles will use the last image posted in that channel as your argument!

She has various commands that allow for deep interaction with derpibooru, such as:

  • ~Derpi
  • ~Next
  • ~Derpitags
  • ~Artist
  • ~Count
